Add a new Hen House
To add a new house entry press the add button.

Fill the entry with the hen detail, fill it correctly and no warning sign displayed then press the save button.

(note: you don't have to fill the population and depreciation/day as it is an auto count)


Edit House Entry
To edit a house entry double click the entry from the list which you want to edit.

Edit the detail then press the save button.


Delete House Entry
To delete house entry, select(single click) the house entry from the list then press the delete button.
