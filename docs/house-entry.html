Overview

To manage hen house data, you will need to use hen house list screen.
Hen house list Screen is located inside master data menu and under hen house tab

Adding New Hen House

To add a new hen house data, please press the "New" button in hen house list screen.

The hen house entry screen consists of several important fields:

  • Name

    Name for the hen house.

  • Purchase Cost

    Total cost spent for purchasing this hen house.

  • Year Usage

    Estimation of total usage in year.

  • Weight

    Weight of this hen house.

  • Hen's Productive Age

    Estimation of total days needed for a hen to be productive.(will be used for other calculation)

  • Active

    Active or not.

  • Population

    Total hen population of this hen house.

  • Depreciation/day

    Depreciation is taken from magic formula:
    Depreciation = Weight x PurchaseCost / Population

Fill hen house fields and ensure there is no warning sign.
Please note that "Population" and "Depreciation/day" field is readonly.
Once you've finished please press Save button

Editing Hen House

To edit a house data, just double click the corresponding house in hen house list screen

Deleting hen House

To delete a house data, please select the corresponding house from the list then press the delete button.
